Xenoblade Chronicles: Definitive Edition

Written by: Rubin Salim

Published 13 - 8 - 2020

Really great game, amazing even. I like the MMO style combat but although at first it can feel a little slow. Thankfully there are some ways to improve the rates but still, if you have shitty luck it'll get annoying real fast. Other than that I don't really have any major gripes with the games. The exploration/moving around could be better. I feel it's way too static. Jumping in this game is so janky. The ability to dash or roll around would be great. With that said, the game has a cool setting and some of the locations are stunning, especially at night time. Just exploring can be really enjoyable with how beautiful the world is. It's surprisingly relaxing sometimes.
Story is pretty good with a solid cast of characters. Has some pretty good twists with foreshadowing. Though there are confusing moments where you question how did that happen, I think those are just standard JRPG tropes. Overall, still enjoyable without getting too annoying. The incredible soundtrack is definitely one of the best things about the game. I'm listening to it while writing this.

Final Verdict: 9.5/10


Xenoblade Chronicles: Definitive Edition is without a doubt the definitive version of the game.
Monolith Soft has successfully made one of the best JRPGs of all time better, and that is no small feat.
Bravo.
