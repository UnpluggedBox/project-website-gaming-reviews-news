Fall Guys Games Review: Easy and fun battle royale games

Written by: Jecky Afrianto

Published 18 - 10 - 2020

UNPLUGGEDGAMES, Jakarta - The world's Jagat games are currently busy discussing the game Fall Guys: Ultimate Knockout. A Battle Royale genre game by Mediatonic and Devolver Digital which was recently released for PC via Steam and the Playstation 4 console. Whether using what concoction, the developer of this game can immediately hook millions of gamers to play the game Fall Guys: Ultimate Knockout in just 24 hours. Not only that, on the Steam platform this game is also the best-selling game this week. And finally, we are curious and want to try playing the game Fall Guys: Ultimate Knockout. We also decided to buy the Fall Guys: Ultimate Knockout game on Steam for Rp. 108,999. Game reviews that use Gamefinity's PC which has mid-end hardware specifications. Apart from being in writing,


Gameplay: 8/10

This point is the main point why this game can sell well in the market. The gameplay of the game Fall Guys: Ultimate Knockout is formulated in such a way as to keep it fun but competitive like most Battle Royale games.

In this game, you will play a cute, funny and adorable character who will fight in various obstacle challenge arenas along with 60 other players. If you are someone who was born in the 90s, you will be reminded of a movie show called Takeshi Fort after seeing the gameplay of this game.

You along with dozens of other players connected online will go through several rounds / obstacles that will be randomly selected. From each of these rounds, of course there will be players who are eliminated for failing to pass the obstacles. Until in the end, there will only be one person left who will be the winner


The Battle Royale concept in the Fall Guys: Ultimate Knockout game is very exciting. Even though the gameplay only passes through obstacles, it's still chaotic because we will elbow each other with other players when passing obstacles. This gameplay brings a mixed atmosphere in the game. Sometimes it annoys us, sometimes it makes us laugh.

Not only single player mode, this game also provides a multiplayer mode which means you can play with your friends in the game Fall Guys: Ultimate Knockout. You can invite your friends directly to play together so that it becomes more exciting and fun.

So that you don't get bored with the characters you play, this game also provides outfit customization options. Starting from color, pattern, face, upper and lower. You can get these customized items for free after leveling up or you can buy them directly through the Store available in the game. You can buy these items with Fall Guys: Ultimate Knockout currency called Kudos and Crown.

Graphic: 9/10
<

The graphics quality of this game is very good. The game developer uses various dominant bright color combinations. These bright colors do not only appear on the side of the characters, the playing arena is dominated by bright colors. This is of course to bring the game atmosphere more fun.

The graphic quality of this game can be adjusted according to the PC specifications you are using. For PC Gamefinity, we tried it with the right alignment setting to get a good experience


Control: 9.5/10

The controls of this game are very simple just like any other casual game. We ourselves tried this game with a mouse and keyboard device. To execute a character, use the WASD keys. To jump using SPACEBAR. To dive, you can use the CTRL button and the SHIFT button to grab. To move the camera can use the mouse.
